- Part 6 -
- create the website -

After successfully enabling GitHub Pages for your repository, the final step involves accessing your website. Once activated, GitHub Pages provides you with a dedicated URL where your project is hosted. Simply click on the provided link and you'll be directed to your live website. This URL serves as the gateway for you and others to explore, share, and interact with the content you published. Whether it is a showcase of your latest project, your first website, or a personal resume, accessing your GitHub Pages website is as simple as clicking the generated URL.
